Ilm Fund was born out of a desire to remove barriers that stand between Muslims and gaining knowledge of their deen.

Ilm Fund provides merit-based and need-based scholarships for programs by:

  • Bayyinah Institute
  • Al Maghrib Institute
  • Al Salam Institute
  • Qalam Institute

These scholarships cover a variety of activities, including in-Tulsa seminars, out-of-town seminars, online study subscriptions (ex. Bayyinah TV, Qalam Now), longer 1-month programs (Qur'an Intensive, Seerah Intensive), year-long programs (Bayyinah Dream, Qalam Seminary), and other special programs (ex. 'umrah trips with scholars). For more information, see our apply page.

Seminars. It'll blow your mind.

Attending a seminar in town? Traveling to catch a class out of town? Ilm Fund has you covered.

Online study. See for yourself.

Interested in furthering your Islamic education but having trouble setting aside the time to travel to seminars? Ilm Fund also covers online study programs like Bayyinah TV, Qalam Now, or AlSalam Institute's remote 'Alimiyyah program.

Programs. Checkmate.

Want to spend your summer studying the Qur'an in depth or invest your winter break in learning about the life of the Prophet ﷺ ? Ilm Fund also covers longer-term, residential programs.

Year-long programs. Checkmate.

For students who truly wish to take their Islamic studies to the next level with year-long residential programs, Ilm Fund is there to help them along their journey. Whether it's Bayyinah Dream or the Qalam Seminary, Ilm Fund is here to help.
